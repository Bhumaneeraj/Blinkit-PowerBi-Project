# Customer Ratings & Feedback Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

---
This project analyzes customer ratings and feedback to understand overall customer satisfaction and identify areas that need improvement.

The dashboard combines star ratings, sentiment (positive or negative), and written feedback to show how customers feel about different service areas such as delivery, customer service, app experience, and product quality.


## Project Objectives
	•	Understand overall customer satisfaction using ratings
	•	Analyze how ratings are distributed from 1 to 5 stars
	•	Compare positive and negative feedback across different categories
	•	Track changes in customer ratings over time
	•	Identify common issues by reviewing negative feedback comments


## Dashboard Features
	•	KPI cards showing average rating and total number of feedback entries
	•	Bar chart showing the distribution of 1–5 star ratings
	•	Sentiment comparison by feedback category
	•	Line chart showing average rating trends over time
	•	Table highlighting the most frequent negative customer feedback
	•	Interactive filtering and cross-highlighting between visuals


## Tools Used
	•	Power BI
	•	DAX for calculated measures
	•	Power Query for data cleaning and transformation


## Key Insights
	•	The average customer rating is moderate, showing that there is room for improvement
	•	Most feedback is in the 3–4 star range, indicating acceptable but not excellent satisfaction
	•	Delivery and Customer Service receive the highest amount of negative feedback
	•	Customer satisfaction remains mostly stable over time
